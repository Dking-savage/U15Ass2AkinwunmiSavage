Empowering the Slovak Community in Manchester

In the vibrant community of South Manchester, a dedicated team of volunteers at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum is working tirelessly to empower and unite the Slovak community in the area. Through a variety of educational programs, cultural workshops, and engaging events, Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum aims to preserve and celebrate Slovak traditions while fostering a sense of belonging among its members.

At the core of their mission is a commitment to providing education for both children and adults who are looking to connect with the Slovak community. Through interactive workshops and educational games, individuals have the opportunity to learn about Slovak culture, language, and heritage in a fun and engaging way.
